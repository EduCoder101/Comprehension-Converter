How It Works

From PDF to online test in four simple steps.

1

Upload Your Paper

Open the Test Generator and upload your reading passage, question paper, and answer key as PDFs.

2

AI Extracts Content

Gemini AI reads your documents and pulls out passages, questions, options, and correct answers automatically.

3

Review & Generate

Check the extracted content, fix anything that needs tweaking, then download the finished test as an HTML file.

4

Share & Analyse

Upload the test to GitHub Pages and share the link. Results flow to your Google Sheet for analysis.
